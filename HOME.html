DISCOVERY

REGINA CAELI SECONDARY OMOR, was found out of the need for the society raise moral upright and well behave citizens in the entire society.
In the year parish council meeting, Rev. Fr. Azigba emphasises on the need of having a high school where students will learn education, morals, sports and ways to impact changes in our society. He said "On my way to the outsation i found 2 boys who are speaking with pains, that they need a starndard education in Omor as those in more developed cities. i felt bad for the two, and took it up to bring it to life. So i present to u a proposed high school which will be named after the Queen of heaven."

On the day of 16th march 2001 the foundation of this great school was laid behind the new CKP church building. And accademic and other activities begins on Oct. 9, of that same year.

On january 6th 2009 the school was moved from its old site to the permanent site now Adjacent to CSS omor, by Rev. Fr. Noberth Chilobe.
